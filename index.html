About Me

Life spent learning is a life well spent. This has been my philosophy ever since I left my roots in Chicago, IL to begin my education at Butler University in 2011. In my time in Indianapolis, I was a devoted student of music and educator of students from all walks of life. From classical violin to progressive metal, my experiences and love of music are influenced by an immense catalogue of artists from around the world.

After spending 3 years as a music educator both at home and abroad, my itch to learn new things could not be satisfied. I began working in the craft brewing industry and fell deep down the rabbit hole of the brewing process and learning from some of the best in the game. Yet with all of these trials and challenges as a beer slinger and music teacher, I yearned for more.

Here in Nashville, I have become a student yet again in perhaps my most ambitious learning challenge to date: become a web developer. What began as a general curiosity in coding led me to love the process of learning new skills and implementing them in web based projects, something that I find profoundly creative.
